Sebastian Civarolo

designer + web developer

houston, tx

I am a multidisciplinary designer with experience in print, environmental graphics, signage, exhibit design, and web design + development. Typography plays a key role in my design aesthetic.

As a self-taught developer, I've built websites for a variety of clients and interactive exhibits for museums. I'm constantly learning and love the challenge of figuring out how to do something I've never done before with code.
