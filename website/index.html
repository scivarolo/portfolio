Sebastian Civarolo

designer + web developer

houston, tx

I am a multi-disciplinary designer with experience in print, environmental graphics, signage, exhibit design, and web design + development. Typography is central to my design aesthetic.

As a self-taught developer, I’ve built websites for a variety of clients including non-profit organizations and small businesses and have also worked on interactive exhibits for museums. I’m constantly learning and love the challenge of figuring out how to do something I’ve never done before with code.
